### Affected products

This advisory is based on the extensions listed below. For these products, we've either been assigned a license by SAGA or purchased it on BuiltByBit.

- Trash Bin
- Minecraft Modpack Installer
- Bedrock Version Changer
- Minecraft Mod Installer

That said, quality issues likely apply to the rest of their product library as well.

### Introduction

SAGA creates loads of modifications for the Pterodactyl panel.
They over standalone Pterodactyl modifications, but also Blueprint extensions.
These modifications are sold on a platform called BuiltByBit.

BuiltByBit often does not conduct code-review in their product review process, hence why extensions like SAGAs often show up on their platform.

Even though these extensions are _no longer_ available on Blueprint browse, we still feel like we have some form of responsibility to call out low-quality extensions.

> Frankly, I'm also done with SAGA blaming Blueprint for a large amount of the issues caused by their addons, trying to completely avoid accountability.

#### Fake reviews

In our research for this advisory, we also came across fake reviews on BuiltByBit aiming to "boost" the rating of "Minecraft Modpack Installer" specifically. These reviews have since been taken down by BuiltByBit, after they had been reported by multiple users.

We've saved screenshots of these reviews, [which are available on the Internet Archive](https://archive.org/details/screenshot-2026-02-19-at-18.40.01).

More recently, more fake reviews started popping up on SAGAs resources. SAGA has since been [temporarily suspended](https://archive.org/details/pasted-image-56_202603).

#### sourceXchange

SAGAs products were removed from [sourceXchange](https://sourcexchange.net) due to machine-generated code concerns. According to sourceXchange, SAGA has removed their downloads from the platform after having their extensions unpublished, leaving all buyers with no downloads.

#### Code quality issues

Overall we've seen various code quality issues in their extensions, often using bash scripts to get things done instead of built-in Blueprint extension APIs, not following [script best practices](/docs/concepts/scripts#important-considerations).

### Conclusion

We recommend against purchasing extensions from SAGA.
We've had quite a lot of community members complaining about their products.

That said, our site offers a [good selection of alternatives to their extensions](/browse), from developers that follow our compatibility and quality standards.
