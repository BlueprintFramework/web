Blueprint has been the home of the Pterodactyl modding community for years.
You've allowed us, and our extension developers, to build powerful modifications on top of Pterodactyl, and carry the open source panel through it's radio-silence in 2024 and 2025.

That's not going away.

Blueprint is all about building software that makes little Timmy's Minecraft server run, and today, we're introducing Hydrodactyl: The game-hosting panel funded by Blueprint.

### First look

Pyrodactyl-oss is now Hydrodactyl.
What was a project operated by Pyro Inc., is now a truly open project under our non-profit organization, thriving for years to come, still maintained by Naterfute.

We're now at Hydrodactyl v6, and we're introducing it with an amazing redesign:

![](/img/blog/hydrodactyl_firstlook.jpeg)

[Amy](https://he3als.xyz/) made concepts for this redesign a while ago, but it was never greenlighted by Pyro. We're bringing it to you anyways, because honestly, it looks really good.

![](/img/blog/hydrodactyl_secondlook.jpeg)
